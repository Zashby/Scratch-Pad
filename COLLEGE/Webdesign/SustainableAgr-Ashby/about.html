Who are we?

We are a dedicated group of horticulture activists with strong feelings regarding the current agricultural practices and societal trends. I developed a passion for plants while working with my wife, a horticulturist by trade. Together, we have worked to change the way society views agriculture by educating our community and encouraging a people to be more proactive in their day to day choices.



Our goal is to teach people the importance of our decisions, our effect on the environment, and how to best treat this planet we all share. We hope that, if nothing else, you come away from our website with a bit more insight into the world around you and what you can do to help! There is a long road ahead to create real progress, but it has to start somewhere so why not here?
